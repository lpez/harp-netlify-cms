FAQ

Do you serve my area?

We serve the Greater Houston area including the cities of Cypress, Spring, Tomball, The Woodlands, Magnolia, Montgomery, Humble, Katy, Sugar Land, Pearland, Missouri City, League City.

Do you also have manual shades?

Yes, all the products are available in manual systems like cordless, continuous cord, retractable cord and more.

What is the best product to darken my room?

Cellular Shades have the smallest deductions, and have room darkening fabrics. Roman Shades are also a good option to darken your room.

I would like to control my shades with my phone, is that possible?

Yes, Hunter Douglas has an app you can download that combined with the PowerView Hub allows you to control Hunter Douglas shades from your phone.
